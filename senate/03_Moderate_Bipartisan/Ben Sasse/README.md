# Senator Ben Sasse - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Management Consulting**: Previously worked as an advisor and consultant for **Boston Consulting Group (BCG)** and **McKinsey & Company**.
- **Higher Education Administration**: Served as the **President of the University of Florida** (2023-2024), where he oversaw significant budget increases, including millions in consulting contracts (notably with McKinsey).
- **Other Roles**: Appointed to the board of **SouthState Bank**. Previously executive director for Christians United For Reformation (CURE) and the Alliance of Confessing Evangelicals (ACE).

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Institutional Influence**: No direct personal lobbying for the "Big Three" found. However, during his Senate career, he was involved in fiscal and regulatory policy affecting major financial institutions.
- **Consulting Proximity**: His work with McKinsey often overlaps with the strategic interests of large institutional investors.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Reformed (Calvinist) / Presbyterian**.
- **Details**: Member of **Grace Church (Presbyterian Church in America - PCA)** in Fremont. Raised in the Lutheran Church–Missouri Synod. Known for an outspoken pro-life stance and advocacy for religious freedom.
