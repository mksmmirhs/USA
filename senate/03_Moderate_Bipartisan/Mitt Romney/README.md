# Senator Mitt Romney - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Bain Capital**: Co-founder and former leader of **Bain Capital**, one of the world's most successful private equity firms.
- **Corporate Turnaround**: Famous for his role in the Salt Lake Organizing Committee for the 2002 Olympics.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Institutional Alignment**: As a giant of the private equity world, his entire professional career is intertwined with the institutional financial system that the Big Three dominate.
- **Legislative Role**: As a Senator, he has worked on bipartisan infrastructure and environmental protection bills.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (Mormon)**.
- **Details**: Former missionary and local lay leader (bishop/stake president). His faith is a central part of his public image and was cited as a driver for his high-profile votes.

## Net Worth
- **Estimated Net Worth**: Approximately **$300M**.
