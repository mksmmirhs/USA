# Senator Pat Toomey - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Finance Industry**: Former investment banker and president of the **Club for Growth**.
- **Post-Senate**: Joined the board of private equity giant **Apollo Global Management** and serves as an advisor to crypto and financial firms.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **ESG Critic**: Publicly scrutinized the "Big Three" for using voting power to advance ESG agendas. Co-authored letters and supported the **INDEX Act** to return voting power to individual investors.
- **Carried Interest**: Vocal defender of the carried interest tax loophole, a key priority for the private equity industry.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Roman Catholic**.
- **Details**: Documented as Catholic.

## Net Worth
- **Estimated Net Worth**: Approximately **$2.6M**.
