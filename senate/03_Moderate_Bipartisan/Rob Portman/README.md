# Senator Rob Portman - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Hospitality**: Owns a significant share of **Lebanon House Inc.**, which operates the Golden Lamb restaurant and hotel.
- **Post-Senate Boards**: Joined the boards of **Bechtel**, **Procter & Gamble**, and the **Atlantic Council** after retiring from the Senate.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Legacy**: Known as a pro-business Republican who spearheaded reforms for 401(k) and IRA plans, the foundational vehicles for the Big Three's asset management dominance. Received significant funding from banks and insurance giants.
