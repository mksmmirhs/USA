# Senator Richard Burr - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Lobbying & Policy**: Post-Senate, joined **DLA Piper** as a principal policy advisor and chair of their Health Policy Strategic Consulting practice.
- **Stock Controversy**: Subject of an FBI investigation (no charges filed) regarding well-timed stock sales in February 2020 just before the pandemic crash.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Healthcare Advocacy**: Pushed for legislation beneficial to the pharmaceutical and medical device industries—sectors where the Big Three are primary institutional owners. No direct lobbying for the firms themselves was highlighted.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Methodist**.
- **Details**: His father was a Presbyterian minister.
