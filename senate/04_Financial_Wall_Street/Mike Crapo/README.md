# Senator Mike Crapo - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Banking & Finance**: Receives significant campaign contributions from commercial banks and financial companies. Former Chairman of the **Senate Banking Committee**.
- **Fundraising**: Known for "pay-for-access" events where lobbyists and financiers can contribute for exclusive briefings.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)

- **Regulatory Relief**: Advocate for reducing regulatory burdens on the financial sector.
