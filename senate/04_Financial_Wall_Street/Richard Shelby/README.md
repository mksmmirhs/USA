# Senator Richard Shelby - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Aerospace & Defense**: Famously secured significant federal funding for Alabama's defense and automotive industries (e.g., Huntsville).
- **Legal Career**: Former lawyer and prosecutor.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Banking Committee**: Formerly chaired the Senate Banking Committee, overseeing the regulatory framework that governs the world's largest asset managers. 
- **Corporate Contracts**: Advocated for major defense contracts (e.g., tanker contracts) for companies where the Big Three are top shareholders.
