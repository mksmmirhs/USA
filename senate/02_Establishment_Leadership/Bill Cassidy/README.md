# Senator Bill Cassidy - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Profession**: Physician (Gastroenterologist).
- **Economic Advocacy**: Active in initiatives with the **Louisiana Association of Business and Industry (LABI)** to support small businesses and oil/gas exploration.
- **Legislation**: Sponsored the *Working Families Tax Cut Act* and provided tax deductions for oil and gas exploration.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **ESG Criticism**: Introduced the **"Integrity in Fiduciary Duty Act"** to prevent asset managers (like the Big Three) from prioritizing ESG factors over financial returns in retirement savings.
- **Accountability**: Demanded answers from the BLS regarding leaks of confidential economic data to individuals at firms including **BlackRock**.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Christian**.
- **Details**: Attends **The Chapel on the Campus** in Baton Rouge. Emphasizes the importance of religious expression and freedom.
