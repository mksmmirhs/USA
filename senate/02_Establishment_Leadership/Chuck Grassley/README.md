# Senator Chuck Grassley - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Agriculture**: Long-time **family farmer** in Butler County, Iowa. His personal business interests are primarily centered on his farming operations.
- **Oversight**: Known as a "taxpayer watchdog," focusing on government spending rather than direct private corporate positions during his long Senate tenure.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Transparency Advocacy**: Co-sponsored the **"Disclosing Foreign Influence in Lobbying Act"** to close loopholes in foreign lobbying disclosure.
- **Market Oversight**: Investigated the **Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA)** regarding insider trading and capital market violations. No direct financial ties to the Big Three found.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Baptist**.
- **Details**: Life-long member of the Baptist faith.
