# Senator Lindsey Graham - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Political Fundraising**: Deep ties to the real estate, healthcare, and banking sectors. Recipient of significant funding from **Turkish interest lobbyists** and organizations.
- **Corporate Backing**: Receives support from major Republican donor networks.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Investment Portfolio**: Disclosed personal investments in **Vanguard** funds, including the *Dividend Appreciation Index ETF (VIG)* and *U.S. Growth Fund (VWUSX)*.
- **Influence Industry**: Criticized for "influence peddling" due to close relationships with various lobbying firms, although no direct legislative lobbying for BlackRock/State Street was found.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Southern Baptist**.
- **Details**: Member of **Corinth Baptist Church**. Known for a dispensationalist theological view that informs his strong support for Israel.
