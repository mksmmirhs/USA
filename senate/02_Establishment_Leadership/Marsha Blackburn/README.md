# Senator Marsha Blackburn - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Marketing & Events**: Founded her own marketing and event management firm prior to politics.
- **Industry Support**: Recipient of significant funding from the **telecommunications (phone/cable)** and **entertainment (film/music)** industries.

- **Lobbyist Influence**: Frequently scrutinized for prioritizing the interests of industry lobbyists, particularly in tech and net neutrality.
- **Big Three Relations**: While the Big Three engage in extensive lobbying on the financial side, Blackburn's specific connections are more heavily concentrated in the entertainment and tech sectors where she has significant legislative influence.
