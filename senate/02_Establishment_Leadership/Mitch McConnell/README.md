# Senator Mitch McConnell - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Corporate Fundraising**: One of the Senate's most prolific fundraisers from **corporate interests**, including **tobacco (Altria)**, **meat processing**, and **defense contractors**.
- **Family Wealth**: Significant net worth (estimated $30M+) largely derived from his wife's (**Elaine Chao**) family wealth (shipping/logistics).

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Citizens United Advocate**: Key figure in relaxing corporate spending limits in elections.
- **Institutional Ties**: While a defender of traditional corporate priorities (tax cuts/deregulation), he has recently criticized "woke" corporate activism influenced by large institutional investors like the Big Three.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Baptist**.
- **Details**: Frequently speaks on religious freedom and judicial appointments.
