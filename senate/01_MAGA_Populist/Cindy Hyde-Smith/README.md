# Senator Cindy Hyde-Smith - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Agriculture**: Strong ties to the **Mississippi farming community**.
- **PAC Contributions**: Receives significant support from PACs representing **farmers, business, and tech interests**.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **ESG Stance**: Aligned with Republican efforts to push back against **ESG policies** promoted by the Big Three. Co-sponsored legislation like the repeal of the natural gas tax and joined an amicus brief against SEC climate disclosure rules.
- **General Lobbying**: Allegations from political opponents regarding contributions from registered lobbyists, though specific ties to the Big Three are indirect through her policy positions.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Christian**.
- **Details**: Her faith is a central part of her identity, particularly influencing her role as chair of the **Senate Pro-Life Caucus**.
