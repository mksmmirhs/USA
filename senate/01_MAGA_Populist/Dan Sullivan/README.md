# Senator Dan Sullivan - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Military & Law**: Background as a **Marine Corps reservist** and attorney.
- **Economic Policy**: Focused on energy development, particularly **oil and gas** in Alaska.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **INDEX Act Author**: Introduced the **"Investor Democracy is Expected (INDEX) Act"** specifically to neutralize the consolidated voting power of **BlackRock, Vanguard, and State Street**.
- **ESG Critic**: Accused the Big Three of "black-balling" Alaskan energy development through ESG-driven shareholder pressure.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Catholic**.
- **Details**: Has advocated for religious liberty protections in federal legislation.
