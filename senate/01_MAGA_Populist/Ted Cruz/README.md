# Senator Ted Cruz - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Legal Career**: Former private practice attorney representing **corporate clients**.
- **Goldman Sachs Connection**: His wife, **Heidi Cruz**, is a managing director at **Goldman Sachs**, which is a significant factor in their financial disclosures.
- **Advocation**: Strong proponent of **business tax cuts** and deregulation.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Lobbying Ban Stance**: Paradoxically supports a ban on former members of Congress becoming lobbyists while receiving significant contributions from PACs representing firms like **Brownstein Hyatt Farber Schreck**.
- **Big Three Influence**: Critics often point to his wife's role at a major bank as an indirect connection to the broader financial ecosystem dominated by the Big Three.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Southern Baptist**.
- **Details**: Son of a pastor; frequently incorporates his faith into his political rhetoric and campaign activities.
