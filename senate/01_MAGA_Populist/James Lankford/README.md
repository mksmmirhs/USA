# Senator James Lankford - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Finance Committee**: Member of the **Senate Finance Committee**, focusing on pro-growth tax initiatives like full expensing for capital investments.
- **Small Business Support**: Recognized by the **NFIB** as a "Guardian of Small Business."

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Direct Engagement**: Has personally **sat down with leadership at BlackRock** to discuss their ESG and social justice investing policies.
- **ESG Critic**: Publicly expressed a preference for firms (like Vanguard) that walk away from ESG investing to focus on fiduciary duties.
- **PAC Funding**: Received contributions from **BlackRock's PAC**.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Southern Baptist**.
- **Details**: An **ordained minister**; views his faith as the cornerstone of his public service and is a staunch advocate for religious liberty.
