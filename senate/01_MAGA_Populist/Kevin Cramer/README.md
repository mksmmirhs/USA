# Senator Kevin Cramer - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Oversight Roles**: Served as a utility regulator before Congress. Member of the **Senate Banking Committee**.
- **Energy Ally**: Strong supporter of domestic energy producers and traditional industry.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **BlackRock Critic**: Questioned the Federal Reserve and BlackRock CEO Larry Fink regarding the firm's role in the **CARES Act** and its support for Chinese companies and "extremist environmental policies."
- **ESG Stance**: Vocal opponent of SEC rules for ESG, labeling them part of a "radical left-wing agenda."

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Evangelical Christian**.
- **Details**: His faith is a significant part of his public and political identity.
