# Senator John Kennedy - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Wealth Accumulation**: Significant net worth (estimated $8M - $22M) derived from long-term investments and assets, notably **mutual funds**.
- **Regulatory Focus**: Strong advocate for small businesses and deregulation.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Anti-Corruption Legislation**: Co-sponsored the **JACK Act**, requiring lobbyists to disclose serious felony convictions.
- **Investment Alignment**: His personal wealth is heavily tied to mutual fund growth, aligning his interests with the broader performance of institutional managers like the Big Three.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Methodist**.
- **Details**: A founding member of his local Methodist church in Louisiana.
