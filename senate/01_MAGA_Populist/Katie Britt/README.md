# Senator Katie Britt - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Industry Leadership**: Former **President and CEO of the Business Council of Alabama**.
- **Lobbying Accusations**: Her husband, Wesley Britt, is a registered lobbyist; she herself was once labeled a "registered lobbyist" in political ads due to her support for the state gas tax while at the Business Council.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Portfolio Ties**: Disclosed investments in major S&P 500 companies (Apple, Amazon, JPMorgan, Nvidia) that are heavily managed and owned by the **Big Three**.
- **Ethics Scrutiny**: Faced criticism for late disclosures of family stock trades (violation of the STOCK Act).

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **United Methodist**.
- **Details**: Openly identifies as a Christian and frequently cites her faith as a driver for her values and public work.
