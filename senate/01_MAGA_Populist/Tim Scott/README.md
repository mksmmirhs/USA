# Senator Tim Scott - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Insurance & Finance**: Operated "Tim Scott Allstate" and worked as a financial advisor before politics.
- **Capital Markets**: Advocate for opening up capital markets and assisting small businesses through his role on the Banking Committee.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Vanguard Holdings**: Financial disclosures (as of late 2025) indicate holdings of **up to $5,000,000** in the **Vanguard Treasury Money Market Investor CL**.
- **Legislative Focus**: As Ranking Member of the Senate Banking Committee, his work directly impacts the regulatory environment for the Big Three.
