# Senator Ron Johnson - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Manufacturing**: Former CEO and owner of **Pacur** (a plastics manufacturer). Sold his stake in 2020 for an estimated **$5M to $25M**.
- **Tax Legislation**: Successfully pushed for changes to the 2017 tax bill that benefited pass-through businesses like his own and those of his major donors.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **ESG Critic**: Publicly criticized **BlackRock** and **State Street** for their support of ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) investing, accusing them of prioritizing personal policy views over retiree security.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Lutheran**.
