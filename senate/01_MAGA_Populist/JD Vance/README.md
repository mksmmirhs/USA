# Senator JD Vance - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Investment Background**: Co-founder of **Narya Capital**; invested in **Strive Asset Management**.
- **Venture Capital**: Background as a venture capitalist and author.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Strive Asset Management**: His investment in Strive (an "anti-ESG" firm) explicitly positions him against the **"Big Three" (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)**.
- **Antitrust Action**: Supports the idea that asset managers could be held liable under antitrust laws for using market power to push "Net Zero" and other ESG goals.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Catholic**.
- **Details**: Converted to Catholicism in 2019; frequently speaks on the importance of Christian values and religious liberty as a bedrock of society.
