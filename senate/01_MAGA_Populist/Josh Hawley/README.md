# Senator Josh Hawley - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Legal and Academic**: Former law professor and advisor for the **Becket Fund for Religious Liberty**.
- **Corporate Critics**: Positions himself as an adversary of **"Big Tech"** and **"Big Pharma."**

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Ending Corporate Influence Act**: Introduced the **"Ending Corporate Influence on Elections Act"** to target the influence of publicly traded corporations and their Super PAC spending.
- **AIPAC Connection**: Received significant funding from **AIPAC**.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Evangelical Presbyterian**.
- **Details**: Vocal advocate for **Christian Nationalism** and the role of faith in public life.
