# Senator Bill Hagerty - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Private Equity**: Co-founded and served as managing director of **Hagerty Peterson & Company**, a private equity investment firm.
- **Consulting**: Started his career with the **Boston Consulting Group (BCG)**.
- **Government Economic Roles**: Former Commissioner of the **Tennessee Department of Economic and Community Development**.
- **Boards**: Served on various company boards, including those on the **NYSE** and **NASDAQ**.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **ESG & Voting Rights**: Vocal critic of the "Big Three" (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street). Co-sponsored the **INDEX Act** and the **Putting Investors First Act** to curb their influence in using shareholder voting power for ESG/DEI agendas.
- **Proxy Advisors**: Criticized the dominance of proxy advisory firms as a "foreign-owned duopoly."

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Episcopalian**.
- **Details**: Identifies as a **conservative Christian**.
