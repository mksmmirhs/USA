# Senator Marco Rubio - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Property Law**: Background in law; previously a registered lobbyist (though he disputed the permanence of this).
- **Campaign Backing**: Heavily supported by **tech, defense, and fossil fuel** industries.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Staffer Influence**: Former Chief of Staff **Cesar Conda** co-founded a lobbying firm representing major corporations like **Oracle and Citigroup**.
- **Big Three Support**: Received significant contributions from lobbyists representing firms like **Goldman Sachs and AT&T**, which are closely tied to the institutional investment world.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Catholic**.
- **Details**: Complex religious history (raised Catholic, LDS for a period, attended Baptist megachurch), now a practicing Catholic.
