# Senator Tommy Tuberville - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Finance & Hedge Funds**: Former partner in **TS Capital**, a hedge fund.
- **Investment Scrutiny**: Noted for active stock trading in sectors related to his committee assignments (Health and Armed Services).
- **Philanthropy**: Founded the **Tommy Tuberville Foundation**, which has faced scrutiny regarding the percentage of funds reaching charitable causes.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Financial Freedom Act**: Introduced legislation to prevent the Labor Department from restricting investment choices in brokerage accounts, aligning with the interests of the broader financial and cryptocurrency industry. No direct lobbying from the Big Three was highlighted.

## Religion

- **Details**: Frequently emphasizes "Judeo-Christian values" in public discourse.
