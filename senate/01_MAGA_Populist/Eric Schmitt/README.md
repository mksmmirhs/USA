# Senator Eric Schmitt - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Legal Oversight**: Former **Missouri Attorney General**.
- **Economic Focus**: Focused on fiduciary duties and the impact of regulations on Missouri industries.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)

- **Anti-ESG Stance**: Vocal critic of the "environmental and social justice movement" influencing industry through the Big Three.

- **Details**: Involved with Jesuit educational institutions; his faith is a documented part of his personal background.
