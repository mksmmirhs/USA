# Senator Joni Ernst - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Small Business Focus**: Chairs the **Senate Small Business Committee**. Proponent of the *Small Business Innovation and Economic Security Act*.
- **Regulatory Reform**: Advocates for reducing regulatory burdens (e.g., WOTUS rule).

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Direct Engagement**: Attended the **BlackRock Infrastructure Summit** in March 2026, engaging with industry leaders and cabinet secretaries.
- **Ethics & Lobbying**: Faced scrutiny over "dark money" ties (Iowa Values) and past relationships with military officials who lobbied her committee.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Evangelical Lutheran**.
- **Details**: Member of the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America.
