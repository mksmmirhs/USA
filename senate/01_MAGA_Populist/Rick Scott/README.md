# Senator Rick Scott - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Healthcare Industry**: Former CEO of **Columbia/HCA**, where he accumulated significant wealth.
- **Financial Interests**: Maintains a "blind trust" which has faced scrutiny. Significant interests in fossil fuel companies.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Institutional Ties**: While no direct lobbying for the Big Three is confirmed, his massive net worth is invested across broad markets. He has co-sponsored legislation to implement a permanent lobbying ban for members of Congress.
- **Vanguard/BlackRock**: No specific direct business connections or lobbying efforts for these firms were identified in recent disclosures, though they are major shareholders in companies he may hold.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Christian**.
- **Details**: His faith is a cited part of his personal life.
