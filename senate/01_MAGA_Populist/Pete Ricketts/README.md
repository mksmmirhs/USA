# Senator Pete Ricketts - Profile

## Business Connections
- **TD Ameritrade**: Former Chief Operating Officer of **Ameritrade**, the company founded by his father.
- **Sports Ownership**: Part-owner of the **Chicago Cubs**.
- **Investment Management**: Founded **Drakon, LLC** to support startups.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Financial Industry Leadership**: As a former high-level executive at a major brokerage firm, his entire career is built on the financial infrastructure where the Big Three are the primary architects.
- **Legislative Role**: Serves on the Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs committee.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Roman Catholic**.
- **Details**: Member of the Knights of Columbus and Knight of the Holy Sepulchre.

## Net Worth
- **Estimated Net Worth**: Approximately **$206.1M** (ranking him among the top 10 wealthiest in Congress).
