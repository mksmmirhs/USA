# Senator Deb Fischer - Profile

## Business Connections
- **Financial Donors**: Received campaign contributions from **BlackRock's PAC** ($2,000 in 2023) and **Paul Singer (Elliott Management)**.
- **Committee Focus**: Serves on the **Agriculture, Commerce, and Armed Services committees**, focusing on infrastructure and national defense.

## Lobbying & "Big Three" Connections (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street)
- **Direct Funding**: One of the few targeted for direct funding from **BlackRock's PAC**.
- **Regulatory Oversight**: As a member of the Commerce Committee, she oversees regulatory issues impacting large financial institutions.

## Religion
- **Affiliation**: **Christian (Presbyterian)**.
- **Details**: States that her faith influences her vision and political thoughts.
